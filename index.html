About Kerala

Kerala, located on the southwestern coast of India, is a state renowned for its lush landscapes, backwaters, and rich cultural heritage. Often referred to as "God's Own Country," Kerala is bordered by the Arabian Sea to the west and the Western Ghats to the east, contributing to its diverse topography and climate. The state is known for its high literacy rate, progressive social indicators, and vibrant traditions in dance, music, and cuisine.
